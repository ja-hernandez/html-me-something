Recipe: Frijoles de olla in the Instant Pot

by Jose Hernandez

INGREDIENTS

      1 pound dried pinto beans (2 cups dry)
      1/4 medium yellow onion, coarsely chopped
      2 cloves garlic
      2 slices of bacon
      Salt (other than kosher)

    Serving options: chopped fresh cilantro, pico de gallo, and/or queso fresco

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Wash and pick through the beans, removing any bits of debris or broken beans, and place them in a colander.
  2. Rinse and drain under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain.
    3. Combine the beans, onion, garlic, and bacon in a 6-quart or larger Instant Pot and add enough water to cover the beans by at least 1 in of water.

    (usually about 4-6 cups)

  3. Seal and cook on the Manual pressure or Bean/Chili setting for 30 min. Let pressure naturally release

    (usually takes 15-20 min)

  4. Check for doneness. Beans should be tender but not mushy or falling apart. If the beans are not done, continue cooking on manual pressure for 5 min.
  5. Remove onion and serve in bowls with desired toppings
